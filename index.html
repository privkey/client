§1 Abstract

PrivKey offers you end-to-end encryption for your communication. It is available as a online service as well as an app for numerous devices. All encryption (and decryption) takes place in your browser before any data is send to (or received from) the internet.

§2 Privacy

It is the overall goal of PrivKey to provide you high level privacy. Therefore we store as less as we can and encrypt as much as possible. Email addresses are the only personal data we store as plain text in order to be able to notify you about new messages.
